# Status Badge Mapping

- `status-backlog`: backlog queue states such as `BACKLOG QA`
- `status-progress`: active work states such as `QA ON PROGRESS`
- `status-new`: new SR intake such as `NEW SR`
- `status-cancel`: canceled SR states
- `status-review`: neutral review or waiting states

Use soft background + strong text contrast. Keep palette limited and consistent with internal UI.
